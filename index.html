Privacy Policy


No data on the user is collected, other than what Android itself collects for every app installed (like crash reports).

The app might store data, related to your usage of the app, locally on your device. The locally stored data is solely for your use and it is never sent anywhere.

Please note that should you have a backup service enabled on your phone, the data from the app might be picked up by the backup service and stored on your backup provider's cloud.

Enjoy the finest privacy.
